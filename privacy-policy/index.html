Privacy Policy

We (Hampton Moore) take your privacy very seriously. To ensure the proper operation of our websites, we collect information that be considered personally identifiable information.

Logging

The web server logs visits to our websites. Information saved includes your IP address, browser, operating system, and the pages viewed. We use these logs to diagnose problems and mitigate attacks from people with too much time on their hands. These logs may be retained indefinitely for analysis of historical traffic.

Google Analytics is used on some of our websites for legacy reasons. We use Google Analytics to see how many visitors come to the website, the approximate location of visitors, how much time they spend on the website, and other aggregate data. To limit the exposure of your information, data sharing is disabled in Google Analytics.

Cookies

We use cookies to help improve your browsing experience on this site. For example, the script that loads the fonts on the page places a cookie in your browser that tells whether the font is already cached in your browser.

Some sites in the Hampton Moore network offer functionality that requires you to log in. In order to stay logged in securely, we need to use a cookie to identify your browser.

Google Analytics may also place cookies in your browser. These cookies are used by Google as described in their privacy policy.

Some websites in the Hampton Moore Network may contain advertisements. These advertisements, served by Google and other approved third-parties, place cookies in your browser to serve advertisements based on your previous visits to the site. Google’s DoubleClick cookie enables advertisements to be served based on your visits to other sites. To opt out of the DoubleClick cookie, you may visit Google’s Ad Settings page. To opt out of other third-party advertising cookies, visit aboutads.info.

By using the contact form, you provide me your name (optional) and email address. This information is kept private and not shared publicly.

Third-Parties

We use a number of third-party services and APIs to help with the proper functioning of this website. Email, anti-spam, content delivery, advertising, and firewalls may be provided by third-parties.

We also may link to or embed content from third-party websites. We have no control over the privacy practices of other sites.

Sharing

Your personal information will not be shared with others except as described above and in cases as required by law.

Changes

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy. Should we make changes to this policy, the date below will be updated.
